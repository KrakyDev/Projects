The Sapori Story

It started with a pot of sugo bubbling on a grandmother's stove in 2012. Chef Marco Bellini spent his childhood watching his Nonna transform humble ingredients into transcendent meals — each dish a conversation between past and present, between memory and imagination.

After training in Bologna, Lisbon, and Tokyo, Marco returned with a vision: a restaurant that honored culinary heritage while embracing the bold flavors of a changing world. In 2018, Sapori opened its doors in Manila's creative district — and never looked back.

Today, Sapori is more than a restaurant. It's a gathering place. A celebration. A reminder that the best moments in life happen around a table. Whether you're marking a milestone or just craving something extraordinary on a Tuesday, we're here — with the finest seasonal ingredients, and a welcome as warm as our kitchen.
